I'm currently a Research Associate at Imperial College London (Apr 2024 – present). My research stands at the AI & Digital Healthcare, with a specific focus on developing robust, interpretable, and accurate AI systems for biomedical research. My research insterest focus Fine-grained recognition, data synthesis, biomarker discovery, data harmonisation, and prognosis. I have published 50+ papers (2000+ citation in total) at top journals including NMI, ERJ, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TMI, MedIA, etc. If you are seeking any form of academic cooperation, please feel free to drop me an email at y.nan20@imperial.ac.uk. 

I earned my PhD at Imperial College London (Jan 2021 – Aug 2024), during which I also served as an Honorary Observer at the Royal Brompton and Harefield Hospital (Nov 2023 – May 2024). Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Ping An Healthcare Technology (Jul 2018 – Nov 2020), where I gained extensive experience in developing clinical AI algorithms for large-scale healthcare applications.
